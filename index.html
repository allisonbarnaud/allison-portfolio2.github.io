Intro

I grew up in Jakarta, Indonesia and went to a French Curriculum school until I moved to Australia in 2012 for University to study Electrical Engineering. During my degree I got my first exposure to programming and quickly developed a passion for it.

Over the next few years I expanded my coding knowledge through various online courses and personal projects.

In 2017 I graduated University with a double major in Electrical and Control Systems Engineering, and immediately started working as an electrical engineer. After 3 years, I decided to explore a different career path and pursue my passion for Software Development.

Work

From a Sushi Chef in Perth to working on a Nickel mine in New Caledonia, I have had a wide range of work experience in the last few years:

I started my engineering career as a Building Services Engineer in 2017, designing electrical infrastructure for landmark buildings around Melbourne. Some of my notable projects include Collins Arch, and Port Melbourne Secondary School.

After 2 years of working in Building Services I decided to explore different engineering industries and found my next opportunity as a FIFO Electrical Reliability Consultant on a Nickel mine in New Caledonia. My role involved providing business improvement strategies and engineering design advice.

During my time off, I continued to learn to code and found myself intrigued by the idea of a career in the Software space. I eventually enrolled into General Assembly's Software Engineering Bootcamp which taught me the fundamentals of full-stack software development. After graduating from General Assembly I was offered my first opportunity at Accenture, where I currently work as a mid-level Software/Devops engineer.

About

I have always been passionate about Emerging Technologies and Engineering: I find myself enjoying 3D printing/3D modelling, electronics designing and programming.


Outside of work I am also passionate about video-games, cooking and fitness.

I am always aspiring to be a part of innovation and change, and believe that working in the Tech space will allow me to reach new heights.
