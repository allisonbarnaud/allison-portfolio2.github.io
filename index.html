Intro

I grew up in Jakarta, Indonesia and went to a French school until I moved to Australia in 2012 for University to study Electrical Engineering. During my degree I got my first exposure to programming and quickly developed a new passion, playing around with programming as a hobby over the next few years.

When I started working in Electrical Engineering I quickly realised that my career aspirations lied elsewhere, and eventually decided to pursue Software Development as a career.

Work

From Sushi Chef to working in a Nickel mine on a pacific island, I have had a wide range of work experience the last few years:

After a brief part-time job as a Sushi Chef, I started my engineering career as a Building Services Engineer, designing electrical infrastructure for landmark buildings around the Melbourne CBD, such as the Collins Arch, and the Port Melbourne Secondary School.

After a while I decided to explore more exciting opportunities and found work on a Nickel mine in New Caledonia as an Electrical Reliability Consultant, providing business improvement strategies and engineering design advice. Unfortunately, this opportunity was cut short: as COVID hit, I decided to return to Melbourne to wait out the storm.

Locked down at home and unemployed, I delved deeper into programming, and decided to enroll into a Software Engineering Bootcamp to build stronger software development fundamentals, which reinforced my passion for coding. After 3 months of intensive learning and 3 more months of job searching I landed my first software development role at Accenture, where I currently work as a software/DevOps engineer.

About

Since childhood I have always had a passion for Technology and making things: Between 3D printing and 3D modelling, electronics designs and now programming.

I am continuously learning and fascinated about Emerging Technologies, and as the world of Software is progressing at an accelerated pace I realised that it is also more accessible than ever, and decided to leap into it and am currently loving it.

Outside of my career I am also passionate about video-games, food and fitness.
